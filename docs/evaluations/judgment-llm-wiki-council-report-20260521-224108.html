LLM Wiki and Graphify Evaluation for Judgment Exploration

Generated 2026-05-21. Scope: Karpathy LLM Wiki pattern, local LLM wiki repository, Graphify, and the proposed judgment ingestion workspace.

Verdict

Use the LLM wiki pattern, but only as a governed doctrine and policing-lessons layer above the judgment corpus. Do not make it the primary storage, ingestion, or retrieval substrate.

Use selected Graphify ideas for graph exploration: confidence-tagged edges, path queries, communities, high-degree nodes, and surprising-connection reports. Do not use Graphify as the legal ingestion engine.

Borrow From LLM Wiki

  • Persistent compiled knowledge.
  • Immutable raw sources.
  • Markdown articles for human and LLM use.
  • Frontmatter schema validation.
  • Citation verification.
  • Human review status.
  • Hierarchical indexes.

Borrow From Graphify

  • Confidence labels on edges.
  • Graph traversal queries.
  • Shortest-path explanations.
  • Community detection.
  • High-degree concept reports.
  • Unexpected cross-cluster links.

Avoid

  • Plain wiki as legal source of truth.
  • Unreviewed LLM-written legal guidance.
  • Generic graph extraction without legal schema.
  • Flattening court-specific doctrine.
  • Optimizing for convictions instead of lawful evidence quality.

Recommended Architecture

1. Judgment Corpus Layer

Authoritative raw judgments, normalized metadata, source manifests, chunks, embeddings, lexical indexes, and ingestion jobs in the separate judgment database.

2. Legal Knowledge Graph Layer

Derived issue, statute, outcome, reason, citation, evidence, and investigation-lapse graph with paragraph-level provenance and confidence status.

3. Legal Doctrine and Policing Lessons Wiki

Reviewed topic articles such as NDPS search compliance, POCSO age proof, hostile witnesses, chain of custody, and court-specific acquittal patterns. Every material claim must cite source judgments and exact chunks or paragraphs.

First Pilot

Start with one issue family: NDPS search and seizure compliance or POCSO age proof. Use Supreme Court plus two High Courts over five years, target 500 to 2,000 judgments, and produce 30 to 50 reviewed wiki articles.

Success requires verified citations for every approved claim, better consistency than raw RAG alone, and coverage-gap logs that identify the next articles to compile.
